How to use

  1. Enter Item, choose Qty & Unit. Optionally add a Sub‑Qty (e.g., 500 g).
  2. Set Price and its basis (e.g., ₹40 per 1 kg, or ₹10 per 500 g).
  3. Click Save Item. Totals update instantly. Use to edit or to delete.
  4. Data is saved automatically in your browser (Local Storage).
  5. Tap Print for a clean bill view. Scan QR to open this page on mobile.
