How to Use

  1. Enter the Item Name and select Quantity & Unit.
  2. Set the Price per unit or portion.
  3. Click Save Item – the total price will update instantly. Use to edit or to delete items.
  4. Enter your Mobile Number to get or send the bill instantly via WhatsApp or download it as a PDF.
  5. Tap Print for a clean bill view. Scan the QR code to revisit this app anytime.
  6. The app will calculate the total cost automatically based on the quantity and price you enter.
  7. Whether you know the price per unit or for a bulk amount, you can easily input your data and the app will handle the calculations for you.

Handling Different Units & Quantities

Some items are sold in bulk or by portion sizes. If you don't know the price per unit, you can easily calculate your total by entering the price for any quantity.

  • Example: ₹100 per dozen → enter the price for 12 units
  • Example: ₹10 for 3 units → enter the price for 3 units
  • Example: ₹50 for 500 g → enter the price for 500 grams

Whether you're buying in bulk or smaller portions, this feature helps you quickly calculate the total cost without manual math.
